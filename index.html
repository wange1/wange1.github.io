When did the Roman Empire Start and End

The Roman Empire started at around 753BC.

It lasted for 700 years before the Byzantine Empire Fell on 1453AD.
At it's peak it covered Greece, Italy, Egypt, Parts of the Middle East and North Africa,
Spain, France, Portugal, Austrian-Hungarian Empire, England etc.

What is the
SPQR

SPQR is an initialism of a Latin phrase Senātus Populusque Rōmānus ("The Roman Senate and People", or more freely as "The Senate and People of Rome"

Roman Empire
